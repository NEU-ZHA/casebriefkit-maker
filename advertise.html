Advertise on CaseBriefKit

CaseBriefKit is a free law school case brief maker and template site. Sponsor placements are reserved for relevant study, writing, exam, and education resources.

Current status

Third-party ads are not running yet. The site is testing ad-ready placements with internal study-resource cards first, then will evaluate AdSense or direct sponsorship after real traffic appears.

This is a pilot sponsor inquiry flow, not an invoice or a traffic guarantee. Do not send payment details through public issues.
