Which free version should you use?

Use the PDF when you want to print a simple worksheet. Use the DOCX when you want an editable file. Use the Word template or Google Docs template when you want copy-and-paste instructions. Use the Obsidian template when your notes are Markdown-based.

What belongs in a case brief?

Most law school case briefs include the facts, issue, rule, analysis, holding, reasoning, and class notes. Your professor may prefer IRAC, FIRAC, CREAC, or another structure, so treat the template as a starting point rather than a fixed rule.
