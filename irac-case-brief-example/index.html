Short IRAC example

Case Name:
Sample Student v. Library

Issue:
Whether a student breached a posted library rule by reserving a study room and not appearing within the required time.

Rule:
When a posted study-room policy requires arrival within a stated period, a reservation may be released if the student does not arrive during that period.

Application:
The student reserved the room for 2:00 p.m. but did not arrive until 2:25 p.m. The policy allowed release after 15 minutes. Because the student missed the stated arrival window, the library followed the posted rule when it released the room.

Conclusion:
The student likely did not keep the reservation under the posted policy.
