Which case brief template should you use?

Use FIRAC when your professor wants facts before the issue. Use IRAC when your class emphasizes issue, rule, application, and conclusion. Use CREAC when you need a more memo-like answer with a conclusion before and after the rule explanation.

If you need a file, use the editable DOCX template. If you want a paper worksheet, use the printable PDF template.
