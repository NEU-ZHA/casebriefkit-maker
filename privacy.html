Privacy

This free static maker runs in the browser. Drafts are stored in localStorage on your device. Do not enter confidential, privileged, or sensitive information.

Third-party analytics are not required for the free maker to run. If analytics are enabled later, they should measure broad usage events such as opening a page, changing a template type, or copying output.

The site is prepared for privacy-focused traffic measurement through optional Cloudflare Web Analytics or Microsoft Clarity snippets, but those snippets should only be enabled after the site owner creates the relevant project token and verifies this privacy notice still matches the deployed configuration.

CaseBriefKit currently uses internal study-resource placements instead of third-party advertising scripts. If third-party ads are added later, this page should identify relevant ad networks, vendor links, and any cookie or personalized advertising choices required by that network.

The site also publishes an ads.txt file for future authorized seller entries. It currently lists no authorized advertising sellers.
