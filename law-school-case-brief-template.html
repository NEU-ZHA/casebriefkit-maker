Law school case brief template FAQ

What is included in the free law school case brief template?

The free template includes fields for case name, citation, court, facts, issue, rule, analysis, holding, reasoning, and class notes.

Can I download the case brief template as PDF or DOCX?

Yes. CaseBriefKit links to a free printable PDF and editable DOCX template.

Does the maker replace reading the case?

No. The maker structures the notes you enter. It does not read the case or provide legal advice.
