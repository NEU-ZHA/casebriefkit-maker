Who this is for

This page is for law students who want a printable form before class or while reading a casebook assignment. The downloadable files are intentionally simple so they can be printed, filled in, or adapted to your professor's preferred format.

When to use the maker

If you want typed notes, use the free case brief maker to copy a FIRAC, IRAC, or CREAC output into Word, Google Docs, Markdown, or Obsidian.
